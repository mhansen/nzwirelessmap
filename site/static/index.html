About

x

This map shows registered point-to-point wireless radio links, with data from the government Radio Spectrum Management database.

You can click on links to get some information about the type of link, and who owns it.

This site was made by Mark Hansen. If you're into that kind of thing, the code's all available on Github. I also wrote a blog post explaining how I made this.

Many thanks to the people at Radio Spectrum Management who make the database available to the public, without which, this site wouldn't be possible. The data is Crown Copyright, with permission to reproduce accurately in any format.

If you have any feedback, criticism, or suggestions, I'd love to hear from you! Leave a comment on my blog, or email me. Cheers!
